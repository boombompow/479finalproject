Undergraduate Programs

Local tools

  •  
  •  
  • Printable version


We offer two undergraduate degrees:

Bachelor of Software Engineering

The BEng program consists of the Engineering Core, the Software Engineering Core, and an Elective Group.

Bachelor of Computer Science

The BCompSci program consists of core courses in computer science, program courses attached to one of eight available options, and technical electives. A Minor in Computer Science is also offered to students in other programs at Concordia.

The Computer Science program may also lead to an Honours program.

Work Experience

Both programs are available in the Co-op format, which alternates work and study terms.



Software Engineering vs. Computer Science?

Find out what makes our undergrad and grad programs in both fields unique and what kind of careers you can expect in each. Read about SoftEng. vs. CompSci.

Graduate Programs

Four Master’s Degree programs are available in Computer Science and in Software Engineering,as well as a dynamic doctoral program and a graduate diploma program. Discover our grad programs.
