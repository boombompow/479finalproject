Important Deadlines

SPRING CONVOCATION

Course option students:
January 15, 2012 is the deadline to submit your graduation application to the Birks Student Service Centre at LB 185 if you will be completing all the course requirements towards your degree by April 30, 2012.
For the Graduation Application go to
http://registrar.concordia.ca/convo/gradapp.html

Master thesis students:

If you wish to graduate this spring, you must submit the first version of your thesis by March 18, 2012 to Ms. Mary Appezzato, Master’s Thesis Coordinator in the Department of Graduate. Your thesis defense will be held two weeks later. Modifications suggested by the master examination committee during thesis defense must be made and approved by your supervisor.
The final version of your thesis must be submitted by April 15, 2012 to Ms. Appezzato and Ms. Edwina Bowen, Diploma Program Assistant in the Department of Computer Science and Software Engineering. Please get in touch with Ms. Bowen for details on thesis submission.
If you are able to meet the deadlines of March 18th and April 15th, submit your graduation application to the Birks Student Service Centre at LB 185 by January 15, 2012. Submit it even if you are unsure that you can meet the first deadline. Once April 15, 2012 arrives and you know that you cannot meet this deadline, then cancel your graduation application at the Birks Student Service Centre.
For the graduation application go to
http://registrar.concordia.ca/convo/gradapp.html

Ph.D. students:

If you wish to graduate this spring, you must submit the first version of your thesis by March 4, 2012 to Ms. Sharon Carey, Doctoral Thesis Coordinator in the School of Graduate Studies. Your thesis defense will be held six weeks later. Modifications suggested by the doctoral examination committee during thesis defense must be made and approved by your supervisor.
The final version of your thesis must be submitted by April 15, 2012 to Ms. Carey and Ms. Halina Monkiewicz, Graduate Program Advisor in the Department of Computer Science & Software Engineering. Please get in touch with Ms. Monkiewicz for details on thesis submission.
If you are able to meet the deadlines of March 4 and April 15, submit your graduation application to the Birks Student Service Centre at LB 185 by January 15, 2012. If you should discover by March 4, 2012 that you are unable to meet this deadline, then go to the Birks Student Service Centre and cancel your graduation application.

Brief description of critical stages and milestones to be achieved during your doctoral study in our department.

For the graduation application go to
http://registrar.concordia.ca/convo/gradapp.html

For Other Important Deadlines, please visit:
http://graduatestudies.concordia.ca/newsandannouncements/importantdates/

 

FALL CONVOCATION

Deadline to submit the Graduation Application form: July 15, 2012.

Further details: http://www.concordia.ca/services/graduation-and-convocation/application/
