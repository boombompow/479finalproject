National Aboriginal Achievement Foundation Mourns Passing of Earl Cook

Toronto, ON  September 19, 2011 – “A life cannot be measured in years, but in what a person leaves behind. Such is the life of Earl Cook. This will stand as an inspiration to future generations in the days ahead,” said Roberta Jamieson, President and CEO of the National Aboriginal Achievement Foundation.
