Part-time Programs

The Part-Time Professional Career Certificate Programs have been carefully designed to provide career minded students with the competitive skills and knowledge required to work in these areas of specialization. These non-credit certificate programs and diplomas are delivered in a flexible and dynamic learning environment which stresses the role of globalization in today's work place.

The Continuing Education Language Institute also offers second language programs in English, French. These part-time non-credit courses are designed to meet the needs of adult learners seeking language training for professional, social and academic purposes. Students are placed into different levels in accordance with their placement test results.
