Full-time Programs

The Centre's Intensive Professional Career Diploma Programs have been carefully designed to provide career minded students with the competitive skills and knowledge required to work in these areas of specialization. These intensive non-credit certificate programs are delivered in a flexible and dynamic learning environment which stresses the role of globalization in today's work place.

In addition, the Intensive (ESL) English program, made up of eight levels, prepares students to enter English-language universities or colleges. Professionals who require a high level of competency can also benefit from the program. Students are trained to read academic, as well as non-academic materials, listen to formal and informal talks, conversations, and lectures; participate in discussions, debates, and seminars; and write letters, papers, and exams, as well as acquire good study skills. Homework and group work are integral to the program.
