Long Distance Access

 

The Class of Service on your telephone determines your ability to dial long distance calls. The classes of service are:

1. Internal calls only

2. Local, toll-free and internal calls

3. Long distance access with authorization code, local, toll-free and internal calls

4. Long distance access, no access to the Bell Canada operator or 411, local, toll-free and internal calls

5. Long distance access, plus access to the Bell Canada operator and 411*, local, toll-free and internal calls

If you currently do not have long distance capabilities on your telephone and required this feature, or if you wish to make a change to your class of service, speak to your department's Telephone Coordinator

*NOTE: Long distance, 411 and Operator-assisted calls are charged to the department where phone is located.
