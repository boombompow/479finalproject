Accessing Your Voice-Mail

On Monday, December 20th, 2010, IITS Voice Services will be on the voice-mail system.


IMPORTANT CHANGES TO NOTE:

• The voice-mail password is now called the PIN
• Voice-mail left on the old system will not be transferred to the new system

The procedure to access the new voice-mail on your telephone is the same way it was on the old system. The only exception is that you will be prompted to enter your PIN instead of your voice-mail password. The PIN is your voice-mail password. There have been little changes to the prompts featured in the new voice-mail system menu. To get a copy of the new voice-mail user guide map, click here: Voice-Mail User Guide 8.0

The Unity Connection voice-mail system allows subscribers of the voice-mail service several ways to access voice-mail messages. They can be accessed from:

• your phone at the university
• another phone within the university
• an outside line
• your email Inbox - IMAP
• your Blackberry or iPhone
• via the web - PCA inbox


ACCESSING VOICE-MAIL ON THE NEW SYSTEM:

From your telephone:

• Press the message key or dial 4220
• Enter your PIN (voice-mail password) followed by #

From another phone in Concordia
• Dial 4220
• Press *
• Enter your ID (extension) followed by #
• Enter your PIN (voice-mail password) followed by #

From outside the university

• Dial 514-848-4220
• Press *
• Enter your ID (extension) followed by #
• Enter your PIN (voice-mail password) followed by #


Accessing Messages on the Old Voice-mail System:

Until January 15, 2011, users will have access to voice-mail messages left on the old voice-mail system.

The way to access the old voice-mail system has changed.

TO ACCESS THE OLD VOICE-MAIL SYSTEM FROM WITHIN THE UNIVERSITY:
1. Dial 8220
2. Press the * button
3. Enter the ID (four digit extension) followed by #
4. Enter the voice-mail password followed by #

TO ACCESS THE OLD VOICE-MAIL SYSTEM FROM OFFSITE:
5. Dial 514-848-2424 ext. 8220
6. Press the * button
7. Enter the ID (four digit extension) followed by #
8. Enter the voice-mail password followed by #
