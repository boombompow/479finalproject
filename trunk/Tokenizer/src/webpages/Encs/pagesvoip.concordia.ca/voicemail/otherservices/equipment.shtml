To Purchase Equipment

The Concordia Computerstore stocks Cisco IP phones and accessories such as headsets, curly cords and patch cords.  Please consult their website at http://ccs.concordia.ca/pricing.html for prices.

When placing an order for a phone, simply submit a request for telephone service to the IITS Help Line by calling ext. 7613 or by sending an e-mail to the IITS Help Line . When you order your phone from the Computer Store, please quote the ticket number given to you by the Help Line.  Please note:  The Help Line will only accept requests from Telephone Coordinators for phone orders.

The phone will be delivered directly to Voice Services for programming and will be installed by one of our technicians.

The Help Line requires the following information in order to create a ticket for your request.


  • First name, last name, email address and telephone number of contact person
  • First name, last name of  telephone user
  • Title and position of telephone user
  • Status of telephone user (full-time, part-time)
  • E-mail address
  • Building, Room #
  • Department Name
  • Budget code
  • Phone type required (model number)
  • Long distance required, yes or no?
  • Voice-mail required, yes or no?
  • Call Waiting, yes or no?
  • Special requirements?
