Licensing

A valid license is mandatory to operate a device on the IP telephony system of Concordia University.  Different licenses are available depending on the type of device, context of use and service features associated with the device.

Licenses are maintained by Concordia University and offered for lease to the community. A one-time licensing fee corresponding to the applicable license type is charged to the operator of a new device. The fee only applies once and to new devices only.  Notably the fee does not apply to replacement devices or devices transferred from a user who left the system to a new user, as long as conditions of use do not change. In any event where a licensing fee may possibly apply, please contact Voice Services to verify whether a new license or an upgrade is required for the phone\device to operate under the proper licensing terms.

The type and whether a license applies gets determined when the service request is processed and appears alongside other applicable service fees in the quote provided to the requestor. Please note that under some conditions, it is possible to upgrade from one license type to another.
