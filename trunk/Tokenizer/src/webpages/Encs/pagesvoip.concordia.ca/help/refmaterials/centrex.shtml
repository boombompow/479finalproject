Centrex III Telephone Features for Single Line Telephone Sets

Reminder: When dialing an IP system phone, press 6 before entering the four4-digit extension.

Call Forward
This feature allows you to re-route your incoming calls to another local or an external number, dependant upon the option selected for you by your department coordinator.

To Call Forward a Call:
• Lift the receiver.
• Wait for the dial tone.
• Dial 555.
• Wait for three(3) beeps, followed by dial tone.
• Dial the destination number.
(For internal calls dial the last 4 digits; for external calls dial 9 plus the 7-digit number).
• Wait for 2 beeps, followed by a period of silence.
• Hang up the receiver.

To Verify That The Call Forward is Working
• Lift the receiver.
• Wait for dial tone.
• Dial your own number. The call is routed to the new destination number. Inform the person at that number that you are forwarding your calls.
• Hang up the receiver.
To Cancel The Call Forward
• Lift the receiver.
• Wait for dial tone.
• Dial 556.
• Wait for 2 beeps, followed by a period of silence.
• Hang up the receiver.
Note: There will be a short ring on your telephone , each time a call is forwarded to an external number. Outgoing calls may still be placed from your telephone when this feature is programmed. If programmed, the ring again feature will ring you back even though your other calls are forwarded.

Call Forward Don’t Answer
This feature automatically routes calls not answered within 3 rings to a pre-determined local.
You will find these pre-determined locals marked on your telephone if this feature has been programmed on your phone.

Call Forward Busy
This feature causes automatically routes calls to a pre-determined local when your line is busy. You will find these pre-determined locals marked on your telephone if this feature has been programmed on your phone.

Call Park
This feature allows you to suspend any call temporarily from your telephone and retrieve that call from another Centrex telephone at Concordia. After parking the call, your set becomes free.

To Park A Call:
• Depress the link key once.
• Wait for 3 beeps, followed by dial tone.
• Dial 575.
• Wait for 2 beeps, followed by a period of silence.
• Hang up the receiver.

To Retrieve A Call:
• Lift the receiver.
• Wait for dial tone.
• Dial 576 plus the local number where the call was left.*
*Note: The local number must be dialed, even if the call is retrieved from the same phone.

Call Pick-Up
This feature allows you to intercept a call ringing at another local within your group.

To Intercept a Call at Another Local:
• Lift the receiver.
• Wait for the dial tone.
• Dial 511.
• Once a click is heard the call has been intercepted.

Call Transfer
This feature allows you to transfer any incoming or outgoing call to another local within Concordia.

To Transfer A Call:
• Depress the link key once. Your caller is now placed on hold.
• Wait for 3 beeps, followed by the dial tone.
• Dial the number to which the call is to be directed and announce that you are transferring the call.
• Hang up the receiver. The transfer is complete.

Note: When the number desired is busy or there is no answer, depress the link key twice. The busy tone or the ringing will be suppressed and you will return tobe back with the initial call/caller.

Call Waiting
This feature advises you that a second caller is attempting to contact you while you are on the line. While on the line with the first call, a short beep tone will be heard indicating that a second call is awaiting your acknowledgement.

To Place the First Caller on Hold and Connect the Second Caller:
• Depress the link key once. The first call is placed on hold and the second caller is connected.

To Alternate Between Call No. 1 And Call No. 2
• Depress the link key once each time you wish to alternate from one call to the other.

To Terminate The First Call Before Responding To The Second
• Hang up the receiver.
• Wait for the ring.
• Respond and your second caller is connected.

Consultation Hold
This feature allows consultation with a third party (internal or external), while the second party is temporarily placed on hold.

To Consult a Third Party:
• Depress the link key once. Your caller is now placed on hold.
• Wait for 3 beeps, followed by the dial tone.
• Dial the third party number required for consultation.
• To return to the first caller, depress the link key twice. You are now in contact with your first caller and the third party is released.
Note: When the number desired is busy or there is no response for a Consultation Hold, depress the link key twice. The busy tone or the ringing will be suppressed and you will be put in contact with the initial call/caller.

Hold
This feature allows you to place a caller on hold.

To Place a Caller on Hold:
• Depress the link key once.
• Wait for 3 beeps, followed by dial tone.
• Dial 533. Your caller is now placed on hold.
• Wait for 2 beeps, followed by a period of silence.
• Hang up the receiver.

To Re-establish Communication:
• Lift the receiver. Your party will still be connected.
Note: A "reminder ring" will advise you at intervals of
75 seconds that your party is still on hold.

Ring Again
This feature allows a caller who encounters a busy internal , CENTREX line to program a call-back from that local when it becomes available.
To Place a Call-Back Using Ring Again:
• Depress the link key once when you hear the busy tone.
• Wait for 3 beeps, followed by dial tone.
• Dial 522.
• Listen for 2 beeps, followed by silence.
• Hang up.

When The Line Becomes Free:
• You will hear an 18 second s ringback cycle, consisting of 1 long ring and 2 short rings, repeated.
• Lift the receiver at any time during this cycle. The ringing at the called local will then be activated.
Note: The feature will be cancelled if you do not respond to the ringback within 18 seconds.

To Cancel The Ring Again:
• Lift the receiver.
• Wait for dial tone.
• Dial 522.
• Wait for 2 beeps, followed by silence.
• Hang up.

Three3-Way Conference Call
This feature allows you to establish a conference call with two other parties, either internal or external.

To Place a Three-3 Way Conference Call:
• Depress the link key once. The first call is now placed on hold.
• Wait for 3 beeps, followed by dial tone.
• Dial the number of the second caller and advise them of the conference call.
• Depress the link key once. The second caller and first caller will be connected and your three- way conference established.
Note: When the number desired is busy or there is no answer, depress the link key twice. The busy tone or the ringing will be suppressed and you will return to the initial call/caller.

For conference calls with more than three participants, see Teleconference Calls.
