Who to contact for help

REQUESTS FOR SERVICES

To order services such as phone, fax and data connections, voice mail and more, please contact your department's Telephone Coordinator.  To find out who the Telephone Coordinator is for your department, click on contact lists below.

CONTACT LISTS

If there is no Telephone Coordinator listed for your department, please email Voice Services .

REQUESTS FOR REPAIRS

To request a repair for an existing service, please contact the IITS Help Line by calling extension 7613 or by sending an email.

In order to process your repair request quickly, please provide the following information:

• Your name

• Phone number

• Email Address

• Office Hours

• Alternate contact person's name and phone number

• Brief description of the problem

In most cases, the repair will be completed within 24 hours.


BILLING INQUIRIES

To inquire about billing, please contact IITS, Administrative Services at 4998, or complete the Billing Inquiries form and a service representative will contact you.   For information on rates and billable services, go to the Rates and Billing section of this web site.
