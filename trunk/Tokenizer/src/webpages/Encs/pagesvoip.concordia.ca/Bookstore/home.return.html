Bookstore Return Policy


You spoke & We listened! After getting your feedback in our online surveys, we"ve decided to change from having a FIVE day return policy up to TEN working days (in other words: two weeks)...

Merchandise may be returned for a full refund within Two Weeks from the date of purchase.
(PS. technically - it's Ten (10) working days... weekends do not count towards those ten days, neither do holidays when the Bookstore is closed.)

Refunds for purchases paid by personal cheque will not be issued before Five (5) working days and not after Ten (10) working days.

Please check with the Bookstore staff for deadline dates for course withdrawal for each semester (the return policy is extended a the start of term for students with proof of the dropped course from the Registrar). Must have cash register receipt.

Note:   For Fall and Winter terms - Students who have dropped a course(s) before the DNE deadline have until the Friday of the same week as the DNE date to return their books for a full refund upon presentation of their course withdrawal form and their cash register receipt.


The following conditions must be met :
  • Must have cash register receipt
  • For items bought with a credit card or debit card, the same card used for the original purchase MUST be provided for charging back a refund.
  • Returned merchandise must be in resaleable condition for a full refund.
    For damaged merchandise a re-stocking charge may be applied.

FINAL SALE for custom courseware material.

FINAL SALE for books shrink-wrapped, containing tapes or software if opened.

FINAL SALE for online access codes (PIN codes, eBooks).

The store manager reserves the right to determine saleability of merchandise.
