Used Book Buybacks:

The Bookstore buys back used textbooks EVERY DAY at the Sir George Williams AND Loyola stores! During regular hours, these Buybacks are conducted at the cash counter near the main entrance of the store. During the Rush, they are held outside the store entrance to avoid the 'chaos' inside.

Unless otherwise noted, Buyback sessions will open 30 minutes after the store opens, and end 30 minutes before the store closes (as per the store hours posted above).


To get an estimate of how much is being offered for your old textbooks, see our Used Book page.
