General Information


Stuff you need to know about the Bookstore such as: Methods of payment, Store Locations, and Shipping Charges...

Locations and Campus Maps
The Sir George Williams (downtown) Campus Bookstore is in the Library Building (LB), and the Loyola (N.D.G.) Campus Bookstore is in Communication Studies and Journalism (CJ) Building (formerly known as the Drummond Science Building).

Maps of the University are available on the main Concordia Web site. See our Contact page to see maps which highlight the Bookstore locations.


Bookstore Addresses and phone numbers are listed at the bottom of each page of this site.


Method of Payment
The bookstore accepts VISA, MasterCard, American Express, INTERAC, and Cash.
(Only VISA, MasterCard, and American Express for online purchases)
We do not accept personal cheques.


Special Services
Bookstore Giftcards and gift certificates are also available in any denomination. The Bookstore also carries MUCTC bus passes and tickets, postage stamps and a wide range of greeting cards.



Also be sure to visit the Concordia Computer Store for your hardware and software needs.


To review the shipping charges for online orders, see our Buy Online information page.
