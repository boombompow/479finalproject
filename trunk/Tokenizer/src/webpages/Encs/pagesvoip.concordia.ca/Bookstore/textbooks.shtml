Want to find our what books you need? Enter your Student ID into our Customized Booklist to see all the books assigned to your registered classed (updated daily). You can also find a book by Course Code using the Course Lookup instead, or list all current textbooks by subject area.

In addition, each term the Bookstores provides used textbooks to students. If available, the used books will be shelved alongside the new texts and are clearly marked with yellow or orange "Used" stickers and are generally 25% below new book prices. If you want to sell your used textbooks, see the Used Books section of our site.

We're also the Bookstore for Marianopolis College, Queen of Angels Academy, and North Star Academy - students at those schools can find your books here.

Are you annoyed by the price of textbooks? Would you like to know how the textbook prices break down?

To try to explain why textbooks are so expensive, below is a chart which illustrates where each dollar out of a textbook's price goes (approximate - the chart represents average costs facing College & University Stores across Canada):

  • 32.3%   Publisher's Production Costs (paper, printing, ancillary materials, and editorial costs)
  • 15.3%   Publisher's Marketing Costs
  • 11.6%   Author Income
  • 11.2%   Campus Store staff (salaries), and personnel costs relating to: ordering, receiving, pricing, shelving, customer service, refunds, etc.
  • 10.0%   Publisher's Administrative Costs (including taxes)
  • 7.0%   Publisher's Income (after tax)
  • 6.5%   Campus Store Income (pre-tax)
  • 4.8%   Campus Store insurance, utilities, building/equipment, rent & miscellaneous overhead
  • 1.3%   Freight
