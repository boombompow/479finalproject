Shopping for used textbooks?

When available, used books will be shelved alongside the new texts and are marked by the yellow or orange "Used" stickers. Used copies will cost 25% less than the new book.

In partnership with College and University bookstores across Canada (through the "SellMyTextbooks.org" buyback network), we offer the following services to make it easier for you to sell your used textbooks. See the Store hours page to see when we are running the Used Book Buy-back, where you will be able to sell us your old textbooks.

Note that we can only buy your used textbooks if you can present your Student ID at the Buyback.

Sell A Book / Buyback Database
Want to know if your books are being bought? Tell us which textbook you want to sell (just enter in its ISBN, usually printed on the back cover next to the barcode) and the website will search the Buyback database to tell you how much we can pay you for them at a Buyback.
 
Buy-Back Alerts / Used Book Classifieds
Do you want to be notified by email when we're buying your books back? Then, sign up for Buyback Alerts. Periodically we will send out emails matching the books you've signed up with the current buyback prices listed in our database.

Is the offered buyback price too low? Or is your book not being bought back at all? Use the Used Book Classifieds to buy and sell books privately between students.


How It Works:

  1. Tell us which books you want to sell
  2. We will check all participating schools (and wholesalers) to find a store currently buying the title.
  3. If no schools are currently buying your title you can sell it to other students directly in our used book classifieds.
  4. We can automatically notify you by Email when a school wants to buy your book if you subscribe to Buyback Alerts.
Used Book Price Check
This will search a database of several Canadian Campus Bookstores (including Concordia) plus two large textbook wholesalers for the best price currently being offered for your used textbook.
