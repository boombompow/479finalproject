Textbook Adoptions

Please note that we have introduced a new Textbook Adoption system.

To access this ordering system, log into your portal at MyConcordia.ca, then browse to the Bookstore section (under "Other Services").

We hope that this new procedure will be more convenient for you (for starters, there's no longer a separate username & password to remember!). Please let us know if you have any feedback or questions. It is a new system and some growing pains are to be expected.

Custom Courseware
Note: ALL aspects of the production of custom courseware (ie. coursepacks, lab manuals) is now being handled internally through the Bookstore.
Copyright Clearance and production are no longer being outsourced to Eastman Systems Inc., therefore all inquiries relating to coursepack production should be sent directly to the Bookstore from now on.

Feel free to ask us questions at: coursepacks@concordia.ca
