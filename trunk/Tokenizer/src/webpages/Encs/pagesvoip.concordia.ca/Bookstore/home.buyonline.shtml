Merchandise Online

Please review this page before shopping online.

To purchase books or select merchandise by credit card, Follow this link to continue to our online store.

If you are a Concordia Faculty or Staff member ordering internal department Stationery or Office Supplies on account, start here instead.

Note that books for courses given at the Loyola Campus (including Queen of Angels Academy & North Star Academy) are only sold at the Loyola Campus Store, and books for courses given at the Downtown Campus (including Continuing Education and Marianopolis College) are only sold at the Sir George Williams store.

Also note that all online purchases of General Merchandise (non-book items such as clothing or other memorabilia) are made through the Sir George Williams Campus web store only.

We now have the option for students to Rent their Textbooks. However, textbook rentals must be done in person. Online rentals are not available.

Another new option is the ability to buy select Online Access Codes (ie Math XL or Lyryx PIN codes) directly online. Unlike the usual method, you will get your access code online immediately upon payment instead of having to wait for Bookstore staff to generate a code for you & email it to you.



When placing your order online, You will be required to acknowledge that you have read and understood the following:

  • Prices and availability may change without notice.

  • Please note that we cannot hold orders in the store to be picked up. All orders will be shipped to the shipping address listed on the order.

  • If you have ordered a USED book and we are out of stock in "USED" we will send a new book and adjust your bill accordingly. We make every effort to provide used books when requested but sometimes they are purchased by "in-store" customers before we can pull them for your order.

  • Quebec residents will have the 7.5% PST added for any General Merchandise items that are part of the purchase. This charge will be reflected on your credit card transaction.

Shipping Charges by region:

  • E-Book only orders: have no shipping charge. E-Book access codes are emailed to you. Mixed orders have normal shipping charges applied to the physical items.

  • Quebec: $10.00 (flat rate)

  • Canada (outside Quebec): $12.00 for the first item, plus $1.00 for each additional item.

  • United States: $25.00 for the first item, plus $1.00 for each additional item.

  • International: Call or email (phone numbers below)

  • EXCEPTION: Due to the special packaging and insurance required for shipping Diploma Frames, they cost $30.00 to Canada or U.S to send the first frame. Add $5.00 for each additional frame shipped to the same address (International shipping will require a custom quote).

  • All charges are in Canadian dollars.

  • Due to the policies of our shipping company (DHL), WE DO NOT SHIP TO P.O. BOXES.

And a bit of advice: if home delivery might be a problem with nobody to answer the courier, maybe think about having your order delivered to your workplace instead.


Payment Options:
The Online Store accepts VISA, MasterCard, and American Express.
