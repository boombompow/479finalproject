Create PDF files with Adobe's Word or Excel to PDF converter

A single click creates a PDF with searchable content that looks like your original. Anyone can view or print your PDF using free Adobe Reader速 software.


Convert from Word or Excel with just one click

In Windows, an Acrobat ribbon in Microsoft Office 2010 makes it easy to create and work with PDF files in Office applications, including Word, Excel, Access, Outlook, PowerPoint, Project, Publisher, and Visio. With a single click, you can create a PDF file, attach it to an email message, or send it to teammates for shared review.

Create enhanced PDF files quickly and easily

With Acrobat X Pro, you can set advanced conversion options in your Office application. Turn Word headings, Excel worksheet names, or PowerPoint titles into navigational bookmarks. Or select other conversion settings in your application, such as attaching source files, retaining links, fitting an Excel spreadsheet on a single page, or preserving PowerPoint animation effects.

Reuse content more efficiently

Acrobat X Pro preserves detailed formatting information when you convert Word or Excel documents to PDF files. This includes multicolumn text, tables, paragraph styles, and comments. You can later convert your PDF file back to a Word or Excel document. Acrobat will preserve formatting details once again, enabling you to begin editing right away instead of wasting time reformatting.
