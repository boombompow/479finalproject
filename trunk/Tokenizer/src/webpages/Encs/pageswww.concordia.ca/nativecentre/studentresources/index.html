student resources

 
 

 

The Centre for Native Education offers support services and resources to First Nations, Métis and Inuit students registered at Concordia. The Centre is a welcoming space where students can meet, plan social activities or just relax between classes.

Staff is available to address individual needs and provide support and encouragement for all First Nations, Métis and Inuit students to continue on their respective journeys and achieve their highest potential at the University.

The following are some of the services available to First Nations, Métis and Inuit students:

  • Academic Advising and Referral
  • Computer Lab
  • Documentation Centre
  • Information and help with scholarships, awards and bursaries
  • Student Lounge
  • Study Space
  • Telephone and Fax
  • Access to a writing assistant
  • Access to an Elder
