scholarships and bursaries

 
 

The following is information on some of the scholarships, awards and bursaries that specifically target First Nations, Métis and Inuit students. We would like to encourage you to apply to scholarships open to the general population as well. You never know…

For an up-to-date listing of Aboriginal scholarships check out the online listing.

For information on other scholarships, awards and bursaries, contact the Financial Aid and Awards Office.

The First Nations, Métis and Inuit Bursaries - Concordia University
Amount:  4 bursaries of $2125 each
Deadline: October of each year

Emergence of Chief - Concordia University
Amount: $500
Deadline: October of each year

Foundation for the Advancement of Aboriginal Youth (FAAY)
Amount:  dozens of scholarships of $2000 to $4000
Deadline:  Mid-October

National Aboriginal Achievement Foundation
Amount:  dozens of scholarships between $1500 and $4000
Deadline:  Various deadlines.

Heroes of Our Time Scholarships

Merck Graduate Student Research Scholarships in Human Kinetics for
First Nation, Métis and Inuit Students
Amount: University of Ottawa, invites applications for two scholarships of $30,000 for two years, to undertake a Master of Science degree (MSc), a Master of Arts (MA) or a PhD in Human Kinetics with special interest in the health of First Nations, Métis and Inuit communities in Canada.
Deadline: None
