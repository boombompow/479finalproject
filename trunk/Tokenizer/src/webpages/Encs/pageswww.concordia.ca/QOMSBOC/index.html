QOMSBOC 2011

 

Welcome!

The Department of Chemistry & Biochemistry at Concordia University is proud to host the 22nd Quebec-Ontario Mini-Symposium on Bioorganic and Organic Chemistry (QOMSBOC).

The conference and banquet will be held at the Sir George Williams (downtown) campus of Concordia University.

We look forward to welcoming you to Montréal!

  • Conference: November 11th-13th, 2011
