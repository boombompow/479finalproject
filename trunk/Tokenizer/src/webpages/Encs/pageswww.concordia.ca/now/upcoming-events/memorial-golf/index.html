Memorial Golf Tournament 2011

Join us for a day of golf!

At the 18th Annual Concordia University Memorial Golf Tournament

When: Wednesday, August 17, 2011 (Rain or shine!)
Where: Hemmingford Golf Club

All proceeds go to the Concordia Memorial Golf Tournament Endowed Bursary

Every year, the Concordia Memorial Golf Tournament Bursary Endowment provides five bursaries valued at $1,000.00 each to students in financial need. Four bursaries are awarded to Undergraduate students (one from each Faculty), and one bursary is awarded to a Graduate student in annual rotation between the Faculties.

Nearly $7,000 was raised at last year's event and with your help, this year will be even more successful. The bursary endowment is growing steadily thanks to the commitment and spirit of giving of Memorial Golf Tournament participants, sponsors, and friends - and this will ensure an ever-increasing number of bursaries for students in future years.

Fun for everyone

All members of the Concordia community and friends are invited to join us for a day of golf (whatever your level of play), brunch, fun, dinner, trophies and prizes!

Course Information

The Hemmingford Sport & Country Club features two 18-hole courses, the Village and Frontiere courses, practice facilities, banquet halls and proshop. The Club opened in 1966. For more information, please consult the club website.

Directions to Hemmingford

  1. From Montreal take the 132 south (Highway 15). Follow signs for the U.S.A. (follow for approx 30 min.)
  2. Take exit # 6 (Hemmingford / Lacolle)
  3. The way to the club is very well indicated, all you have to do is follow the blue signs.
  4. At the flashing light in the village of Hemmingford turn left. We are located approx. 1.5 KM from the flashing light. You will see the driving range and practice facilities on your right.
