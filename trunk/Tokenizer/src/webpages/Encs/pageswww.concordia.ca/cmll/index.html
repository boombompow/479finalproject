Are you intrigued by the ancient world? Captivated by different languages? Fascinated by how language developed in the human brain? The Department of Classics, Modern Languages and Linguistics (CMLL) offers a unique opportunity to study one or more of seven disciplines.

Please note that the Department does not offer translation services or the certification of translated documents.

MA in Hispanic Studies

Unique in Canada, the MA in Hispanic Studies program offers an integrated second cycle education that interweaves translation, language pedagogy, and literary and cultural studies.

The MA in Hispanic Studies Lecture Series brings a renowned scholar in Hispanic Studies to Concordia each semester. In March 2010, Dr. Keith Ellis (Emeritus Professor, University of Toronto) presented "Evolving Trends in Caribbean Studies" [full lecture on YouTube].
