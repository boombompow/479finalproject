NMR facility

 


Instruments

Department Chemistry & Biochemistry of Concordia University maintains and operates two solution NMR spectrometers:

Varian VNMRS-500 MHz: 5 mm AutoX DB (Dual Broadband) probe 1H-19F/X[15N-31P], with z-PFG, operating with VNMRJ 2.3 software under LINUX Red Hat 5. Automatic tuning for all nuclei by ProTune accessory.

Varian INOVA-300 MHz: two 5 mm probes - direct SW 1H/X[15N-31P] and inverse ID 1H/ X[15N-31P], with z-PFG, operating with VNMR 6.1 software on SUN Solaris 8. Probe is typically pre-tuned to proton and phosphorus frequencies.

In addition, for biomolecular NMR, a Varian 800 MHz spectrometer can be used at Quebec/Eastern Canada High Field NMR Facility.



Recently installed Varian NMR 500 MHz spectrometer.


 
 


Service and local manuals

Including most of the routine experiments:

1D NMR: 1H/13C/31P/19F/15N/17O, solvent suppression, 13C-spectra editing: DEPT/APT

2D homonuclear NMR: COSY/TOCSY/ NOESY/ROESY

2D heteronuclear NMR: HSQC/HMQC/HMBC.

A clear solution sample is required for service in 5 mm NMR tube with a volume of around 0.6 mL (a height of at least 4 cm).

Policy

We offer NMR services to both University and external users. A service charge will be applied for each use of the NMR facility service, which includes use of the spectrometer or training. Please enquire for more details.

Faculty, post-docs and students who have undergone user training may operate the instruments without direct supervision.
