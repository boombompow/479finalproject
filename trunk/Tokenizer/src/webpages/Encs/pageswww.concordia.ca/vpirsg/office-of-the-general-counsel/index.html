Office of the General Counsel

 

The Office of the General Counsel is responsible for the provision of all legal services to the University. The legal team is as follows:

Me Frederica Jacobs, General Counsel
Head of the Office of the General Counsel and responsible for the overall operations of the OGC as well as all major legal dossiers.

Me Eva Samartzidis, Associate General Counsel
Primary source of legal advice and expertise to University administrators with respect to intellectual property, research matters and purchasing matters amongst other dossiers.

Me Melodie Sullivan, Legal Counsel - Student and Administrative Affairs
Primary source of legal advice and expertise to University administrators with respect to student-related and general administrative law issues amongst other dossiers.

Me Geneviève Desmarais, Legal Counsel - Employment and Labour Relations
Primary source of legal advice and expertise for the Human Resources Department and University administrators with respect to labour law issues amongst other dossiers.

Me Valérie Gastebled, Legal Counsel - Facilities Management
Primary source of legal advice and expertise to the Facilities Management Department and University administrators with respect to all property and real-estate related matters amongst other dossiers.
