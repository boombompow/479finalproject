June 1, 2011, Concordia President Frederick Lowy meets with Quebec government senior officials.  Click here for more information.

The External Relations section has been expanded to provide the University Community with information about and links to the three levels of government and their services and to other external stakeholders.

The Associate Vice-President page provides a biography of Associate Vice-President, External Relations, Russell Copeman.

What do we do? gives an overview of the mandate of the External Relations Office.

The About Our Governments section provides you with information regarding the Government of Canada, the Government of Québec and the City of Montréal.
