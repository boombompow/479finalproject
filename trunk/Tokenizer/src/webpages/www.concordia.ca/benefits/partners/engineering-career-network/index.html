Engineering Career Network

 

Concordia Engineering graduates with more than two years of experience are entitled to many advantages and free services from this employment portal. Not only can they find work opportunities for professionals and executives in engineering, but they can also take advantage of the career management tools as well as the free and confidential services from HR experts.

For Graduates

Job Search

  • View job postings

Free Career Management Services

  • In-person or telephone interview with one of our HR experts;
  • Help with writing your résumé;
  • Expert advice on the job market, employment possibilities, current salaries, etc.
  • Exclusive access to a secured job portal;
  • Possibility of receiving job alerts or conference announcements;
  • Preferred rate to attend our career management conferences throughout the year.

Online Career Management Tools

  • Build your contact network through reference interviews
  • How to write your résumé
  • The strategy behind your job search
  • Salary negotiation: do’s and don’ts
  • Six basic principles when preparing for an interview

We regularly update ECAC Careers so visit often to take advantage of changes and improvements. For more information, please visit our website or contact the Engineering Career Network at: 514-789-4478.

For Employers

Job Postings

By posting offers on ECAC Careers, you reach a large and exclusive bank of high level professionals in engineering who have an average of ten years experience. Competitive rates apply.

For more information contact the Engineering Career Network: 514-789-4478.
