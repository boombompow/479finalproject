The John Molson School of Business Alumni Chapter supports and promotes the interests of JMSB alumni at all points in their careers and facilitates their personal and professional development.

The chapter aims to contribute to the JMSB’s reputation and the value of all degrees granted by the school.

President, Adriana Molluso, MBA 09

Adriana currently works as a lawyer for Blake, Cassels & Graydon LLP, specializing in commercial real estate law. Before joining Blakes, she acted as Senior Legal Counsel (Real Estate, Corporate and Commercial Law) for The Standard Life Assurance Company of Canada. 

She is a member of the Québec Bar Association, Quebec Young Bar Association and CREW Montreal (Commercial Real Estate Women).

Send your event suggestions to Michelle Grostern, Alumni Officer, Faculties, at michelle.grostern@concordia.ca or 514-848-2424, ext. 4978.
