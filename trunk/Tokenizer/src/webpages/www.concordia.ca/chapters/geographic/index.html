Volunteer for your chapter!

We're seeking volunteers to help organize events in our chapters around the world. Getting involved is a great opportunity to network as well as a meaningful way to give back to your alma mater.

For more information, please contact Lina Uberti, alumni officer for geographic chapters, at lina.uberti@concordia.ca.

Surprised to find a Concordia representative in your part of the world? Don’t be! 

To serve the more than 25,000 alumni living outside of Quebec, Alumni Relations cultivates 11 geographic chapters in eight regions.

Our goal is to develop activities and offer services that foster mutually beneficial, lifelong relationships with alumni. Our job is to increase the number of graduates who feel a strong allegiance to Concordia University.

Geographic Chapters and Regions thrive because of our dedication to Concordia’s mission. Its success is directly related to enthusiastic alumni who volunteer as ambassadors leading and building strong networks around the world.

We intend to build on past successes by expanding current networks and research new regions.

To get involved, contact Lina Uberti, Alumni Officer, Geographic Chapters/Regions at lina.uberti@concordia.ca or 514-848-2424 ext. 4606.
