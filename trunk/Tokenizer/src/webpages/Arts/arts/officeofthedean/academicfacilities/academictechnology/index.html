Academic technology

Local tools

  •  
  •  
  • Printable version
 

The Faculty of Arts & Science's Academic Technology Unit is a service centre providing technical support and instructional and creative services to the Arts and Science community.

A complete list of our Technology and Design Services can be found in the "Faculty and staff services" section of this site. We offer a wide range of services, including computer support, technical training, web design, graphic design, and instructional design support for technology in the classroom. We also offer server support for teaching and research in the Faculty.

Academic Technology has five full-time people on staff. Their expertise comes from a variety of educational backgrounds, including communication, journalism, educational technology, engineering, sociology, computer support and network management, and they share a lively interest in education.
