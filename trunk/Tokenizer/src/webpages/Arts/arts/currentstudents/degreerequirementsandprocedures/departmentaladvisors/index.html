Departmental advisors

Local tools

  •  
  •  
  • Printable version
 

All departments have advisors that are available to assist students. Reasons to see your department advisor may be the following:

  • Academic Performance Regulations (GPA)
  • Course selection
  • Difficulty with courses or program
  • Elective grouping enrolment
  • Entry into restricted courses
  • Grading policies
  • Graduation requirements
  • Late Registration and Withdrawal
  • Participation on Student Exchange
  • Permission to take courses at institutions outside Quebec
  • Course substitutions
  • Signature for Student Requests

Department Advisor schedules vary. Students should contact their departments for more information on their advisor’s schedule.

Academic advising may be required for some students before registration. Please consult the Arts & Science advising information section of the registration guide.
