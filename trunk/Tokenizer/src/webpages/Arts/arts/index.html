Welcome

 

Concordia University's Arts and Science Learning Centre (ASLC) was inaugurated in May 2001 to serve as a state-of-the-art facility for courses using advanced multimedia technologies.

The ASLC is located on the fourth floor of Hall Building (Sir George Williams campus), and the Central Building (Loyola campus).
