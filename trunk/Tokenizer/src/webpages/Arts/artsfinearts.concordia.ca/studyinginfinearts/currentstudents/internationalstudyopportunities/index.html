Make a difference!

The Concordia Volunteer Abroard Program (CVAP) is a non-profit humanitarian organization created with the purpose of providing assistance to communities worst hit by HIV/AIDS, poverty and war. Every year, the program sends students to Uganda where they work on a variety of sustainable development projects.

 



An exciting feature of any university education is the contact students make with individuals from around the world. However, nothing compares to actually discovering the world for yourself!


International Student Exchange Program (ISEP)

This program allows students to study abroad for a semester or two at one of Concordia's partner universities. These universities have exchange agreements with Concordia, which allows students to study abroad while continuing to pay Concordia tuition.


Study Abroad Program

Students interested in studying at an institution that does not have an exchange agreement with Concordia may still be granted permission to do so. In this situation, students would be required to pay tuition directly to their host university. In order to be considered for this option, students must submit a Student Request Form to the Office of Student Affairs.



An adventure is waiting for you! Visit the Concordia International website for more information.
