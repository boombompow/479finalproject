Features

  • Navigation (Beta)

    Get free, turn-by-turn GPS navigation for driving, walking, and public transit directions.

    Watch video Learn more

  • Indoor maps (Beta) New!

    View maps and your accurate location inside select airports, shopping malls, and retail stores.

    Watch video Learn more

  • Places

    Find nearby places with the Places icon. Rate places to get personalized recommendations.

    Learn more

  • 3D Maps

    Explore the map in 3D. With two fingers, drag down to tilt and see 3D buildings where available; twist to rotate (on supported devices).

    Watch video Learn more

  • Compass Mode

    Enter 3D compass mode to orient yourself and automatically rotate the map and labels to whichever direction youâ¬"re facing.

    Watch video Learn more

  • Offline Reliability

    Maps for the areas you use the most are cached in the background so you can see and interact with the map, even if you lose your connection temporarily.

  • Directions

    Get driving, transit, biking, or walking directions in a list or on the map (where data is available).

  • Place Pages

    Search for any business or place to find listing info, pictures, reviews & ratings, and more rich details.

  • Latitude

    See where your friends are right now. Share your location or check in at places to let friends know where you are.

    Watch video Learn more

  • My Location

    See your location on a map, even if you don't have GPS.

    Watch video

  • Traffic

    Real-time traffic helps you find the fastest route.

  • Street View

    View street-level imagery of businesses and turns in directions.

    Watch video

  • Layers

    Overlay layers on the map (e.g. Traffic, Satellite, Terrain, search results, and more).
