Features

  • Updates

    View friends' updates, scraps and photos and interact with them.

  • Your Profile

    Access your profile, scrapbook and photo albums.

  • Birthdays

    Stay on top of your friends' current and upcoming birthdays.

  • Photos

    Take new photos and upload them directly to your Orkut albums.

  • Friends

    Browse all your friends profiles, scrapbooks and photo albums.

  • Friendship requests

    Quickly accept or reject new friendship requests.
