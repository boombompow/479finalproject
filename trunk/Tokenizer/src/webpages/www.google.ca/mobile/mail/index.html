Gmail for mobile

Try Gmail for mobile and get your email wherever you are.

How to get the Gmail app

Send Gmail app to your phone
  • Visit m.google.com/mail on your phone
  • Download from Android Market
  • Download from iTunes

Features

  • Search

    Enter keywords to find the exact message you're looking for. It works just like Google search.

  • Conversation view

    Replies are grouped in a single thread, making it easier to understand the context of a message.

  • Stars and labels

    Use stars to indicate a note is special. Organize your email with labels.

  • Archive, mute, move

    Tidy up your inbox with fewer clicks.

  • More features

    Offline access, spam filtering, keyboard shortcuts, address auto-complete, and many more!

Note: Not all features are available on all phones. For more information, see the Help Center.
